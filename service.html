What servicing is there on an electric car?

When it comes to servicing an electric car, the major areas checked include the electric motor(s), battery pack, high-voltage electric cabling, suspension, brakes, steering, wheels, tyres and lights.

What do electric cars provide?

Battery-electric cars don't use any gasoline, but instead run solely on electricity stored in a battery pack that energizes one or more electric motors and produces zero tailpipe emissions. These cars can be charged most anywhere, anytime and usually at a much lower cost than fueling with gasoline.
